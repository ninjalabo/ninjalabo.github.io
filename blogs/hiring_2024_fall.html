Software Engineer - AI Model Compression & DNN Runtime Development

  • Location: Remote (Occasional meetings at A-Grid, Otaniemi)
  • Company: NinjaLABO
  • Duration: Full-time or Part-time for 3 months (ASAP to the end of December).

About Us:

NinjaLABO is a fresh, finnish startup at the forefront of AI innovation, focusing on AI model compression and deep neural network (DNN) runtime optimization for IoT, particularly for enabling onboard Edge AI in satellite systems.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Implement DNN model compression techniques.

  • Integrate the above into our SaaS platform, TinyML as-a-Service.

  • Port and optimize DNN runtimes for Edge devices such as Nvidia Jetson and Orange Pi 5+.

Requirements:

  • Strong proficiency in Python and C/C++.
  • Familiarity with PyTorch or TensorFlow.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Hands-on experience with Nvidia Jetson & Orange Pi 5+.
  • Experience in AI model compression and DNN runtime development.
  • Knowledge of TensorFlow Lite for Microcontrollers, MLIR, and IREE.
  • Knowledge of HyperSpectral Imaging (HSI).

Preferred Education:

  • Successful completion of CS-E4890 - Deep Learning D with good grades.
  • Successful completion of CS-E4580 - Programming Parallel Computers D with good grades.
